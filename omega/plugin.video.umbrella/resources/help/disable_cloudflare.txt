
By disabling [B]"CloudFlare Protected Providers"[/B] you are turning off the folowing scrapers that use cfscrape to make requests.

  maxrls (hoster)
  rapidmoviez (hoster)
  rlsbb (hoster)
  scenerls (hoster)
  extratorrent (torrent)
  limetorrents (torrent)
  torrentapi (torrent)
  torrentgalaxy (torrent)

Scraping of these sites will not take place with this option enabled.
